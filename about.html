About Us

Mei Mei's Cafe  

Our Story

Mei mei is an affectionate term for the younger female member in a family. It is a term I was frequently called by my family growing up and still sometimes today. "Mei mei!", my grandma or mom would shout when bringing home a hefty paper bag of my favorite Chinese pastries. Growing up, these Chinese cafes were near and dear to my heart. The incredible flavors and memories I shared with my brother are artifacts of nostalgia I will forever cherish deeply   . Therefore, I want to forever encapsulate this important part of my childhood through opening up this Cafe and sharing it to the people of New York. So that you guys can also experience the amazing memories I've had through these foods and drinks and learn more about Chinese culture through them.               

 

our goal

Mei mei's bakery is an Asian-American, woman-owned cafe and aims to preserve Chinese culture. Making it a safe space for people of all ages, backgrounds, immigration statuses and identities.
